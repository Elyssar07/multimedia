Integration Technology and Extended Reality (AR/VR)

Integration technology is increasingly complemented by Extended Reality (XR), which includes Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR). These technologies are transforming how we interact with the digital and physical worlds.

Augmented Reality (AR): AR combines virtual elements with the physical environment, overlaying digital information onto the real world. It is used in mobile apps, games, marketing, education, and more.

Virtual Reality (VR): VR immerses the user in a completely virtual environment, providing an immersive experience. It is used in games, simulations, training, therapy, and more.
